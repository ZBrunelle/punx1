Atomic Punx Freestyle Skis

Pro level park skis

Atomic Punx started being manufactured by Atomic in 2010 and were featured on numerous ski websites like Freeskier Magazine and newschoolers.com as being the best park ski of all time. Since then they have been used by numerous pro skiers like Jossi Wells and Khai Krepela and remain one of the best park skis available on the market. Each year brings a new design and updated features like more durable materials or carbon bands to add durability and pop.

Features of the 2017 Punx

  • Park Rocker (10/80/10)
  • Step Down Sidewall 2.0
  • Resist Edge 2.2
  • New Look
